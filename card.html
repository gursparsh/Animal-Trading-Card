Psittaciformes(Parrot)

There are around 372 different parrot species

  • Scientific Name:: Psittaciformes
  • Height: ranges from 3.5 to 40 inches
  • Average lifespan: 40 yrs
  • Habitat: Southern Tropical Areas

Parrots, also known as psittacines, are birds of the roughly 393 species in 92 genera comprising the order Psittaciformes, found mostly in tropical and subtropical regions.
